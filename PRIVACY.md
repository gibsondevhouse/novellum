## 1. Data We Collect

**Novellum does not collect any personal data.**

All your writing data — projects, scenes, characters, outlines, world-building notes, and preferences — is stored locally in a SQLite database on your device. No data is transmitted to Gibson Dev House or any third-party analytics service.

## 2. Local Storage

All application data is stored in a SQLite database file on your device. You have full control over this data. You can back it up, move it, or delete it at any time. No data is synced to the cloud.

## 3. AI Features

When you use AI features in Novellum, the content you submit (such as scene context) is sent to **OpenRouter** over HTTPS to process your request. This data is governed by OpenRouter's own privacy policy.

- OpenRouter Privacy Policy: <https://openrouter.ai/privacy>

Your OpenRouter API key is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to Gibson Dev House.

## 4. Analytics

Novellum collects **no analytics or telemetry**. There is no crash reporting, usage tracking, or behavioral analytics of any kind.

## 5. Third-Party Services

The only third-party service that receives data from Novellum is **OpenRouter** (when you use AI features). You control this by choosing whether to enable AI features and by providing your own API key.

## 6. Data Security

Your data is stored on your device under your operating system's standard file permissions. We recommend keeping your operating system and device secure. Backup archives (`.novellum.zip`) are stored locally and are never uploaded by Novellum.
