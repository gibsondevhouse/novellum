- Stage 003 complete; entity card and form primitives exist as shared components.

## Exit Criteria

- All eight entity families use one `EntityGallery` layout, one `EntityCard`, one `EntityDetail` layout, and one `EntityForm` layout.

- Gates pass; visual QA evidence captured for at least three representative entity families.
